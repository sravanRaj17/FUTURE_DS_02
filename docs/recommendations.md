\# Recommendations to Reduce Customer Churn



\- Encourage customers to shift from month-to-month contracts to annual or long-term plans by offering discounts or incentives.

\- Improve the onboarding experience during the first 3 to 6 months to reduce early customer churn.

\- Introduce targeted offers or loyalty programs for customers with high monthly charges.

\- Promote auto-payment and paperless billing options to improve customer retention.

\- Identify high-risk customers early and engage them with personalized communication and support.

\- Provide additional value-added services to increase customer engagement and long-term retention.
